About Us

Scro provides an escrow service to PointCoin merchants.  An escrow service ensures that the customer and the merchant's agreements are upheld by acting as an intermediary wallet between the customer and the merchant.  With this service, Scro hopes to create a safer marketplace for PointCoin.  Scro charges a 10% transaction fee for our services.
